## Tone of Voice

- **Professional but warm** — clinical product for clinicians
- **Confident, not arrogant** — AI assists, never replaces
- **Nigeria-first** — built for Nigerian hospitals, not adapted from global solutions
- **Plain language wherever possible** — reflects the product philosophy
- **NDPR compliance as a strength** — not a checkbox, a differentiator
